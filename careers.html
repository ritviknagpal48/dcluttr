Hello, job seeker.

From the cochlear implant to the iPhone, what humans create can excite, uplift, and shape the lives of millions. At Dovetail, our mission is to enable a small part of that by helping our customers create incredible products through the power of customer insights. We exist to improve the quality of every thing.

It starts with our team, our work, and the culture we curate. We aspire to excellence and nothing is too small to be improved. We’re proud of what we make, and we expect you to be proud of what you make, too. We sweat the details on a small product change to a major marketing campaign. We believe working in-person fosters creativity, teamwork, and innovative problem-solving, and encourage our team to push themselves, to learn more, and to seek greatness in their craft. If you’re smart, talented, and motivated, and thrive in a high-performing, collaborative, and accountable environment, Dovetail is a great place to work. But you will also need to be humble and self-reflective, because feedback is delivered consistently and constructively every day—not just in formal check-ins. If you prefer a rigid process and corporate structure, that’s not us. We all get shit done and ship fast.

Apply if you value excellence and are never satisfied with mediocrity; give (and receive) positive and constructive feedback on work; thrive in situations of uncertainty and ambiguity; sweat the details on everything you produce; embrace solving hard problems with smart colleagues; think and write clearly and concisely, deploy storytelling to drive buy-in; remain calm in stressful situations; and seek what’s best for customers at all times.

If this doesn’t resonate, please don’t apply as you won’t succeed. But, if you are excited by our culture of excellence, passionate about our mission, and keen to learn and master your craft, scroll down to read more.

Dovetail exists to improve the quality of every thing

Through the power of customer insights, we help teams worldwide create better products, better experiences, and better services for the public to rely on. We know our mission is ambitious and challenging—this is by design. Because hard things are worth doing.

Learn more about the development of our mission

Our values aren’t window dressing

We put the customer first—from quickly fixing a bug to solving their most difficult problems. We’re open by default, sharing financials, board notes, shareholder updates, and encouraging everyone to be curious to understand the decisions we make. If you’re sick of working at a snail’s pace—you will love it here. We do the thing, shipping quickly and pragmatically. From the placement of a comma to the copy you’re reading, if we do it, we aim to nail it. Last but not least, we remind ourselves to stay humble. We work together, ask questions, and commit to self-improvement.

Put the customer first

Seek to understand—and then solve—real customer problems. Earn and keep customer love through transparency, authenticity, and results. Watch competitors, but obsess over customers.

If we do it, we nail it

First impressions matter. Sweat the details, don’t sacrifice quality, and create an awesome experience at every touchpoint whether it’s support, our interview process, or the product.

Do the thing

Keep the speed up by taking pragmatic risks. Only agonize over irreversible decisions. Be reliable and follow through. If you say you’ll do something, do it. If you’re off track, communicate.

Stay humble

Be authentic, humble, and empathetic to others. Remember the human behind the work. Operate collaboratively—rally around problems, and celebrate wins together.

Open by default

Trust others and assume good intent. If you need to build context and understanding; ask. Always share information freely.

How we show up

These guiding principles help us live our values on a day-to-day basis.

Curiosity

Creativity comes from diversity, divergent thinking, and open-mindedness. Celebrate ideas from left-of-field places. Respond first to ideas with positivity about the potential, before skepticism.

Efficiency

Solve problems with scalable, compounding solutions. Continually interrogate our ways of working, tools, process, and rituals to look for optimization opportunities.

Fundamentals

The best solutions often come from consistently “nailing it.” If we do this, we’ll maintain customer trust and be proud of what we create.

Initiative

Being ‘open by default’ gives everyone the opportunity to build context and clarity, take initiative, make autonomous decisions, and influence their own career path.

Ownership

Act like an owner. Equity is at a company-level, not a team-level. No functional bickering.

Progress

We balance all inputs to “do the thing.” Disagree and commit over making decisions by committee. We’re intentional about who’s in the room to facilitate effective and productive conversations.

Storytelling

We use the power of emotive storytelling to align, inspire, and provide clarity—both for internal communication as well as outward-facing brand and product positioning.

Teamwork

Working together as a team builds trust, relationships, a sense of belonging, and results in better outcomes. The most effective way to achieve this is in-person collaboration.

Life at Dovetail

Dovetail isn’t for everyone—and we’re okay with that. If you’re ready to be challenged, make an impact, and grow alongside a diverse team, this is the place for you.

What to expect

An ambitious mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of every thing. This ambitious goal presents an exciting opportunity for those looking to make a real difference in people’s lives.

Innovation and speed

We’re at the forefront of technology, constantly pushing boundaries to stay ahead. Join a fast-paced environment where your ideas—and hard work—shapes the future.

Customer-centric focus

Understanding our customers is key to delivering an exceptional product. Join a curious team that puts customers at the center of everything we do.

Collaboration and diversity

We love diverse perspectives. Join a culture where individuals from myriad backgrounds work together, bringing their unique skills and ideas to the table.

Challenges and growth

The road to success is under construction. We want resilient folks who thrive under pressure, embrace the unknown, and see obstacles as growth opportunities.

Quality and endurance

We love quality products. If you have a passion for creating exceptional, long-lasting solutions that make a tangible difference, you’ll feel right at home with us.
