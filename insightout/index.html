The talks

Get ready to be inspired

Deciding what to build next with Jen Taylor

Building a successful product is a complex balancing act between establishing customer trust, delivering expected value, and innovating on new problems. Jen Taylor is here to give us an organizational perspective on navigating this dynamic terrain. If you’ve ever wondered how executive leaders think about balancing customer needs with business outcomes—this talk is for you.

Jen Taylor

Investing in insights infrastructure for product teams

When we talk about insights work, we’re often focused on activities: interviews, concept tests, reports. But shouldn’t we be spending a little more time talking about insights infrastructure? In his talk, Behzod Sirjani will draw a contrast between qualitative insights work today and data science of yesterday to plot out a path forward to more customer-centered product development.

Behzod Sirjani

Behavioral audits for better outcomes

Qualitative research is a powerful tool for product teams of all types, but it does have one sizable drawback. It leaves you to infer causation and impact. However, if we were to combine our qualitative insights work with behavioral science methods, we can start to quantify the impact of those insights. Christelle covers exactly this in her talk on leveraging behavioral audits.

Christelle Ngnoumen, PhD

How PMs should be leveraging customer insights

Too often, there seems to be a disconnect between the workflows and timelines of product managers and leaders and those in insights work. Frankly, the root cause of this may be a lack of understanding of how to leverage insights professionals. Claire Menke Priestley has been a senior leader on both sides of the aisle as Director of Product at Meta and previously a Director of Research.

Claire Menke Priestley

Beyond specialization: the generalist revolution that will shape the future of insights

Over the past few years, we’ve seen a massive increase in specialization across a variety of roles. However, according to John Garvie, that’s not going to last. This means everyone across the product org is going to need to get more comfortable talking to and learning from customers. To walk you through why that’s the case and how to adjust your career development accordingly, tune in to John’s talk.

John Garvie

A tale of two ventures: How UX strengthens your venture’s lifecycle

When building a new company, it’s easy to focus on the numbers. Sign ups, churn, revenue. However, those numbers are outputs of great insights work and great design. In her talk, Claudia contrasts two stories that show how deeper customer understanding, and better design, lead to meaningfully different business outcomes.

Claudia Natasia

From the anatomy of an insight to the business of research

What’s the difference between an observation and an insight? Should researchers recommend or do they simply provide learnings? And what responsibility do insights professionals have when it comes to generating revenue? Join Eniola Abioye, UX Researcher @ Meta and UXR Career Coach as she takes us from the anatomy of an insight through to a renewed vision for UX Research.

Eniola Abioye

Prix fixe to a la carte: tailoring insight delivery across an organization

Drowning stakeholders in research overload? Ditch the one-size-fits-all approach! In his talk, Jared Forney will explore Okta’s framework for delivering curated, fast insights and deep-dive experiences within the same system. Learn how to empower users to find what they need on their own terms and leave ready to design a dynamic system that saves time and satisfies everyone’s appetite.

Jared Forney

FAQs
What is Insight Out?

Insight Out is a one-day conference by Dovetail happening on April 11, 2024 in-person in San Francisco and online around the globe. We’re gathering a community of customer-obsessed folks from product, design, research, and beyond to come together to connect, discuss big ideas, and learn over a fun-filled, action-packed day.

How do I join?

In-person: Tickets to join in-person in San Francisco are now available! Click the “Get tickets” button on this page to grab your ticket.

Online: Alternatively, you can join us for free online from wherever you are. Please click the “Get tickets” button on this page and select “Online” to stream via YouTube.

Who is the event for?

Insight Out is for customer-obsessed people who want to build better products and deliver better services for the world. We’re planning a multidisciplinary experience. Whether you’re in product, research, design, CX, or another field entirely—if you want to learn more about harnessing the power of customer insights—this event is for you.

Is Insight Out free?

If you’re joining online—yes! If you’re joining us in-person in San Francisco, tickets start at $199 USD. Wherever you’re joining from, you’ll need a ticket. Please click the “Get tickets” button on this page to register your details so you don’t miss out!

Will Insight Out be recorded?

Yes, it sure will be! Make sure you register your details above by clicking the “Get tickets” button on this page so we can send you all the good stuff post-event.
