Privacy Policy

Last updated20 September 2024

At Dcluttr, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our information collection and use practices, including how we handle Personal Data.

1. Information Collection

Dcluttr collects information from users of our website, including Personal Data, for marketing, security, administration purposes, and in fulfilling our service commitment to you or as required by law.

By providing Personal Data voluntarily, you consent to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy and acknowledge that such Personal Data may be transferred to servers and offices of Dcluttr globally.

2. Why We Collect Information

Information is collected to enhance your user experience, provide information regarding Dcluttr services or related offers, and fulfill your requests for products and services.

Most commonly, we collect your Personal Data when you create a Dcluttr account. This information is used for access, security, and billing purposes.

3. Data Protection

We implement various security measures to protect your Personal Data. Personal Data is stored securely and accessible only to authorized personnel. If necessary, any Personal Data shared with third parties will be kept confidential and used only for the intended purpose.

4. Information Disclosure

Dcluttr does not sell or share your data with unrelated third parties, except to provide the services you've requested or under specific circumstances, such as:

  • To respond to subpoenas, court orders, or to establish or exercise our legal rights.

  • To investigate or prevent illegal activities, suspected fraud, or threats to physical safety.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Dcluttr uses cookies and pixel tags to understand site traffic and user behavior. We do not store Personal Data in cookies or pixel tags.

6. Data Confidentiality and Brand/Agency Separation

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of your data. Dcluttr ensures that the data collected from each brand or agency is stored completely separately, with no overlap or access between different brands or agencies. We follow industry-leading data storage and security practices to ensure that your data remains isolated and secure.

Strict protocols are in place to safeguard against unauthorized access, ensuring that no data is compromised or shared without explicit consent. Your data is protected using robust encryption, firewalls, and other security measures that comply with international standards, ensuring full confidentiality at all times.

7. Access and Management of Personal Data

You can view, modify, or delete your Personal Data at any time by logging into your Dcluttr account. Additionally, you may request to access or delete your account information via our contact methods below.
