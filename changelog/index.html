Changelog

All the latest Dovetail features, product updates, and squashed bugs.

Posted 10 March 2024
Upgraded canvas experience

We’ve improved one of our most popular features - canvas view. Now, when you create a new canvas, it will start as a blank space where you can add, remove, and duplicate the data you want to work with.

To add objects to your canvas, select Add in the left-hand toolbar or press P on your keyboard. From there, you can narrow down results using search and filters.

Additionally, objects can now be duplicated on a canvas so you can map a single piece of data across multiple groups and themes.

Learn more
Posted 4 March 2024
Follow all projects within a folder

You can now effortlessly follow all projects in a folder with just one click. Stay up-to-date with your team’s projects by navigating to a folder and clicking 🔔 Follow in the top right.

You can also select where you receive notifications by navigating to your notification settings.

Learn more
Posted 29 February 2024
Create reels directly from your canvas

We’ve made it easier for you to share reels with your team. Simply select two or more video highlights from your canvas view and press Play reel.

You can give your reel a title and select whether you’d like to include captions, then all you need to do is download and share with your team!

Learn more
Posted 13 February 2024
Enhanced transcription summaries

We’ve made further improvements to note summaries! When you summarize a note that includes a transcript, the result will be split into sections with automatically generated titles and timestamps. This update provides more context to summaries and allows you to quickly identify and navigate to the exact points in the transcript where the summary came from.

Learn more
Posted 28 January 2024
Create highlights without tags

You can now create highlights without needing to apply a tag. Simply select text on a note like normal, and click Highlight from the new, simplified action bar. You can still apply tags to highlights later on, either from the note itself, or from a highlight view.

This change makes it faster than ever to share a quick clip or assemble a highlight reel from a customer call, sales demo, or research session.

If you’re a power user, you can quickly create highlights by selecting text, pressing Tab on your keyboard, then pressing Enter.

Learn more

Admins can go to Settings → Beta to enable redaction.

Posted 6 November 2023
References are now static, point-in-time snapshots

We’ve made a significant improvement to how references behave in Dovetail. Adding a reference to an insight will now create a point-in-time snapshot of the object, so that if the underlying data is modified or deleted, it will remain unchanged.

You can now be assured that the content within your insights is just as you intended them to be, even if the original has been modified.

Additionally, this change also brings faster loading times for your insights and quicker search results!
