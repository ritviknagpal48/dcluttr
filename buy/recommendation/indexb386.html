Data security

Keep data safe and secure

Our platform is backed by industry-standard security, reliability, and privacy controls to ensure that your data is available and secure.

OpenID-based SSO

Single sign-on support via OpenID Connect, available in leading identity providers.

Access control

Manage user roles and control individual permissions on folders and projects to protect confidentiality or participant privacy.

EU & USA data hosting

New workspaces can choose to host their data in Europe or the United States to comply with local data regulations.

SOC 2 Type II

Established mitigation of risks related to security, availability, and confidentiality.

Optional HIPAA add-on

Our optional HIPAA solution provides access controls and features to help manage PHI, such as advanced restrictions on sharing, access, and data exporting.

Encrypt in transit and rest

All data is encrypted in transit via TLS 1.2 and at rest with AES-256 encryption.

Managed users

Ability to provide and revoke access to roles, and manage active user sessions.

JIT provisioning

Leverage domain-restricted sign up to provision user accounts as they need access.

Domain allowlisting

Restrict user provisioning to verified email addresses at your approved domains.

GDPR-ready

Dovetail adheres to the EU’s GDPR legislation on storage and deletion of user data.

Security reviews

Regular data backups, testing, and encryption, along with other cloud security techniques.
